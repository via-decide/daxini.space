Privacy Policy

Alchemist — Chemistry Learning Platform · Last updated: 15 July 2026

1. Introduction

Alchemist ("we", "our", or "the App") is an educational chemistry application developed by Dharam Daxini, operating under the brand Via Decide. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Alchemist mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Alchemist is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data:

  • No personal information: We do not require account creation, login, or any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number) to use the App.
  • No location data: We do not access or collect your location.
  • No camera or microphone access: The App does not use your device's camera or microphone.
  • Local storage only: Your learning progress, quiz scores, and preferences (such as theme settings) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to external websites (e.g., official GATE, JAM, and TIFR past paper PDFs hosted on government and institutional domains). These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices. We do not embed any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks in the App.

4. Advertising

Alchemist does not display any advertisements. We do not integrate any ad networks or ad-related SDKs.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties. Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to share.

6. Data Security

All application data (progress, settings, scores) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage. No data is transmitted over the internet except when you voluntarily open external links to view past paper PDFs.

7. Children's Privacy

Alchemist is an educational application intended for users aged 13 and above (typically undergraduate and postgraduate chemistry students). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not require registration or collect personal data, no age verification is necessary.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
