# Thales of Miletus

**The First Philosopher**

| | |
|---|---|
| Era | 624–546 BC |
| Origin | Miletus, Greece |
| Field | Natural Philosophy |

Thales is widely regarded as the first philosopher in Western tradition. He proposed that water is the fundamental substance of all matter and predicted solar eclipses, pioneering the shift from mythological to rational explanation.
