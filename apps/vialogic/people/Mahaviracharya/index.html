ACT I: THE JAIN COURT
   
       

The Rashtrakuta Kingdom

       

Karnataka, c. 850 CE

       

In the lush Deccan Plateau of southern India, the mighty Rashtrakuta Empire was at the peak of its cultural glory. Its capital hummed with scholars, poets, and astronomers. King Amoghavarsha — himself a poet and philosopher — was the greatest patron of learning of his age.

   
   
        HISTORICAL INSIGHT: Under Amoghavarsha's court, the sciences flourished like nowhere else in the medieval world.
