# René Descartes

**The Architect of Reason**

- **Era:** 1596–1650
- **Origin:** La Haye, France
- **Field:** Philosophy, Mathematics

Creator of the Cartesian coordinate system, Descartes bridged geometry and algebra. His "Cogito, ergo sum" remains the starting point for modern rationalism.
