# Plato

**The Architect of Reality**

| | |
|---|---|
| Era | 428–348 BC |
| Origin | Athens, Greece |
| Field | Metaphysics & Political Philosophy |

Plato founded the Academy, the Western world's first institution of higher learning. His Theory of Forms proposed that the physical world is a shadow of a higher, perfect reality accessible only through reason.
