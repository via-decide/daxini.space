Diogenes of Sinope

The Cynic in the Barrel

412–323 BC
Sinope, Turkey
Cynicism

Diogenes lived in a barrel, carried a lantern searching for an honest man, and told Alexander the Great to move out of his sunlight. He rejected all conventions and lived by nature alone.
