Adi Śaṅkara

The Unifier of Vedanta

788–820 CE
Kerala, India
Advaita Vedanta

Śaṅkara consolidated the doctrine of Advaita (non-duality), arguing that Brahman alone is real and the world is Maya (illusion). He established four monasteries that endure to this day.
