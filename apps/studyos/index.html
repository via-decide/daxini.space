StudyOS

Modular Learning Engine • Offline Study Environment

Education software is broken. Most modern study tools are wrappers for cloud databases designed to keep you online, showing advertisements, or selling certificates. StudyOS is different. It is a local-first learning environment that functions completely offline inside your browser sandbox.

The Concept Map vs. Linear Lists

We teach knowledge as a list of lessons. Knowledge is actually a topological map. StudyOS replaces the traditional linear scroll with a concept matrix that charts dependencies and prerequisites.

Active Recall Under Constraints

Instead of feeding you AI-generated summaries that require zero cognitive load to read, StudyOS forces active extraction:

  • Grounded Verification: Flashcards and logic cards are evaluated locally against preloaded datasets.
  • Zero Leakage: Your study progress, scores, and active topics are saved directly to IndexedDB. They never leave your machine.
  • P2P Sharing: Synced nodes can export custom cards in raw formats without requiring login layers or cloud directories.
