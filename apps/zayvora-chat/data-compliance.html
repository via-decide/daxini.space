DPDP 2023 Compliant GDPR Compliant CCPA Compliant Local-First Architecture

Our compliance posture is simple: We don't collect your data. Your learning data lives on your device, under your control. This is not a policy decision — it is an architectural decision. The system was designed this way from the ground up.

1. Architecture Overview

VIA Connect uses a local-first architecture. This means:

  • All computation happens on your device
  • All data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB and localStorage
  • No user data is transmitted to any server for processing or storage
  • No cloud synchronization exists
  • No user accounts are required for core functionality

This architectural choice eliminates entire categories of compliance risk by design, rather than by policy.

5. How to Exercise Your Rights

Access Your Data

Open the VIA Connect application. All your data is visible within the app. Use the Library panel to browse your session history and content blocks.

Export Your Data

Use the built-in export features to download your data in PDF, EPUB, or .zay format. This satisfies the right to data portability.

Delete Your Data

Clear your browser's site data for the VIA Connect domain, or uninstall the application. Since no copy of your data exists on our servers, this permanently deletes all your data.

Contact the Data Protection Officer
