Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: India

CONSTITUTIONAL STATEMENT

VIA Connect does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. We cannot — because we never see it. Your data lives entirely on your device.

What We Collect

Nothing. Zero. The answer is no data.

VIA Connect is architecturally incapable of collecting your data because:

  • There is no user account. No username, no email, no phone number is ever entered.
  • Your cryptographic identity is generated locally on your device using the Web Crypto API. The private key never leaves your device.
  • All messages are stored in IndexedDB — your browser's local database. They do not sync to any cloud.
  • All files are stored in the Origin Private File System (OPFS) — local disk, device-only.
  • Peer-to-peer connections are established directly between two browsers using WebRTC. Messages travel device-to-device, not through our servers.

The Signaling Relay

To establish a WebRTC connection, two devices must first find each other. VIA Connect uses a Cloudflare Workers signaling relay for this handshake only.

The relay sees: two anonymous WebSocket connections in the same room. It forwards SDP/ICE packets blindly. It does not log them, store them, or inspect them. Once WebRTC is established, even the relay is bypassed — traffic flows directly between devices.

The relay stores no messages, no identities, no metadata. Rooms auto-expire after the WebSocket closes.

Encryption

All messages are encrypted end-to-end using AES-GCM with keys derived from your locally generated identity pair (ECDH P-256). Even if our relay were compromised, all it would see is an encrypted blob it cannot read.

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare Workers — Hosts the signaling relay. Subject to Cloudflare's privacy policy. No user data is sent.
  • Google STUN servers — Used for NAT traversal (standard WebRTC practice). Only your IP address is briefly used to negotiate the connection. Not logged by us.
  • jsDelivr CDN — Delivers Dexie.js and QRious.js libraries. Standard CDN delivery, no user data transmitted.

Your Rights Under DPDP Act 2023

The Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 grants you rights over your personal data. Since we collect none, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end.

To delete your data: open VIA Connect → Profile → Wipe Local Node. This deletes your identity, contacts, and all messages from your device instantly. We have no copy to delete.

Children

VIA Connect does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children. The architecture ensures this is technically impossible.

Changes to This Policy

If we change how the app works in a way that affects data handling, this policy will be updated with a new effective date. The app is open for inspection — architecture claims are verifiable in the source.
