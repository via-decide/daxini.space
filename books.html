About This Stack

The Daxini Stack is a 5-volume blueprint for sovereign AI infrastructure. It describes the architecture, operations, and distribution of systems that are fully owned and controlled by the operator.

Built on principles: Local-first execution (no remote dependencies), deterministic behavior (reproducible outcomes), kernel immutability (frozen execution basis), and sovereign distribution (no platform dependency).
